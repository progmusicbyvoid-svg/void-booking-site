Press Kit

Short Bio

V( )ID is an instrumental metal band with progressive influences. They create a synthesis of crushing rhythms, heavy drums and an ever changing duality between order and chaos. With unusual song structures as well as catchy riffs they create a sonic journey throughout their songs.

  • RIYL: modern prog, djent, cinematic post-rock
  • Stage time: 30–50 min (adaptable)

Stage & Power

  • Stage size: 4 m x 3 m minimum
  • Power: 4× Schucko 230 V on stage (1 for each band member)
  • Monitoring: 3 monitor mixes
  • Backline preference: amps, cabs, drums if provided
  • Changeover time: 20 min (left-handed drummer considerations)

Legal: The technical rider forms part of the guest performance contract. If minimum requirements are not met on show day, we reserve the right to cancel the performance; agreed fee and travel costs remain due.
